Platform Features

Everything your forest
operation needs

From satellite-powered growth monitoring to automated EUDR compliance — Silvamind replaces fragmented tools with one intelligent platform.

🛰️
Satellite Forest Monitoring

Continuously analyzes satellite imagery (Sentinel-2, Planet Labs) to track forest health, growth rates, storm damage, and bark beetle risk across all your land parcels in one dashboard.

🪓
AI Harvest Optimizer

Predicts the optimal time to harvest each stand by combining timber volume estimates, real-time market prices, weather forecasts, and growth models to maximize your return per hectare.

📊
Timber Marketplace & Price Intelligence

Live timber price tracking across buyers and regions, with an integrated marketplace that matches sellers with verified buyers — replacing opaque phone-call-based trading.

📋
EUDR Compliance Automation

Automatically generates geolocation-tagged traceability documentation required by the EU Deforestation Regulation, ensuring every timber lot has a verifiable deforestation-free origin.

🌱
Forest Carbon & Biodiversity Scoring

Calculates carbon sequestration estimates and biodiversity indicators for each forest parcel, enabling carbon credit opportunities and ESG reporting for institutional forest owners.

🚛
Logistics & Harvesting Planner

AI-optimized scheduling for harvesting crews and timber transport, accounting for road conditions, seasonal ground frost windows, and mill delivery slots to minimize costs.

How It Works

From forest parcel to
profitable timber sale

Silvamind connects your land data, market signals, and compliance workflows into one seamless system.

1

Connect Your Parcels

Upload your forest parcel boundaries or sync with national cadastral databases. Silvamind immediately begins satellite monitoring and generates baseline timber volume estimates for each stand.

2

Get AI-Powered Insights

Receive continuous alerts on growth milestones, storm damage, pest risks, and optimal harvest windows. The AI cross-references live timber prices and climate models to surface the best opportunities.

3

Sell with Full Transparency

List your timber on the marketplace with automated EUDR documentation. Verified buyers compete for your lots, ensuring fair market pricing and complete traceability from forest to mill.
